# Technical Challenges & Open Problems: V31

While the architectural logic of Genesis Titanus is sound, several theoretical bottlenecks require advanced simulation to solve.

### 1. The Sintering Kinetics Bottleneck
*   **Challenge:** Cellular-level crystallization of carbon into black diamond (Carbonado) typically requires immense geological pressure.
*   **Open Problem:** Refining the biological enzyme path borrowed from *Patella vulgata* to catalyze carbon-sintering at ambient pressures without destroying surrounding organic tissue.

### 2. Micro-Turbine Mechanical Wear
*   **Challenge:** Molecular phase-change turbines converting $150^\circ\text{C}$ waste heat are subject to massive kinetic stress.
*   **Open Problem:** Developing self-healing lipid/graphene interfaces to ensure the turbines don't suffer structural fatigue over millions of metabolic cycles.
