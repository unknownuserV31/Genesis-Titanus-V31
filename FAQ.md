# Frequently Asked Questions: Genesis Titanus (V31)

### Q1: What is the primary purpose of Genesis Titanus?
**A:** It is a theoretical framework designed to map out a "Zero-Failure" biological life-form, combining extreme material science with advanced genomic resilience to survive highly hostile conditions.

### Q2: How does the system handle extreme heat ($150^\circ\text{C}$)?
**A:** Instead of suffering cellular denaturation, the energetic metabolism uses Thermoelectric ATP Turbines to absorb and recycle the extreme thermal energy, turning waste heat back into metabolic power.

### Q3: What makes the skeletal structure unbreakable?
**A:** The platform utilizes the Carbonado Bone Matrix, a concept inspired by limpet teeth biology that sinters metabolic waste carbon into a resilient black diamond matrix at a cellular level.

### Q4: Is this project open-source?
**A:** Yes. It is licensed under the Titan-Open-Logic (TOL-1.0) framework, allowing open collaboration, distribution, and theoretical development.
