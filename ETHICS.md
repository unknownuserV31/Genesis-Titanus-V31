# Ethical & Safety Framework: TOL-1.0 Alignment

The extreme resilience engineered into Genesis Titanus (V31) requires strict containment and philosophical boundaries to ensure systemic alignment.

### 1. Biological Containment Vectors (The Kill-Switch Gating)
*   **Nutrient Dependency:** The Lin28a hyper-regeneration matrix and Carbonado sintering pathways require a highly specific, synthetic non-natural amino acid catalyst to function. 
*   **Absence Cascade:** In the absence of this external synthetic compound, the Thermoelectric ATP Turbines enter a locked dormancy state, safely idling the platform's advanced modules.

### 2. Strategic Non-Aggression Protocols
*   **Defensive Specialization:** The Sputnik-Phage layer is hardcoded strictly as a reactive, predatory matrix against foreign invading pathogens. It contains zero genetic pathways for proactive cellular toxicity against non-threatening host ecosystems.
