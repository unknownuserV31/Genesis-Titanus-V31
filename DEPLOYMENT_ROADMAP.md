# Project Genesis Titanus: Deployment Roadmap

This document outlines the theoretical phases required to simulate, test, and realize the Genesis Titanus biological platform.

## Phase 1: Material Synthesis & Simulation
*   **Objective:** Model the Carbonado Bone Matrix.
*   **Focus:** Simulating limpet-inspired biological mechanisms to sinter waste carbon into black diamond structures at a cellular level.
*   **Milestone:** Complete structural integrity stress-tests via digital physics engines.

## Phase 2: Thermodynamic Energy Capture
*   **Objective:** Formulate the Thermoelectric ATP Turbines.
*   **Focus:** Mapping the energy conversion of 150°C metabolic waste heat back into usable cellular energy (ATP) using bio-engineered Seebeck variants.
*   **Milestone:** Achieve steady-state thermodynamic recycling models.

## Phase 3: Genomic Encoding & Immunity
*   **Objective:** Integrate the Sputnik-Phage and Diamond-Encased Protection.
*   **Focus:** Programming autonomous, benign virophages to neutralize external biological threats while safeguarding core DNA with molecular shielding.
*   **Milestone:** Zero-failure autonomous defense simulation against known viral vectors.

## Phase 4: Hyper-Regeneration
*   **Objective:** Activate the Lin28a Axolotl Matrix.
*   **Focus:** Scaling blastema formation pathways for rapid, full-scale tissue and limb replacement.
*   **Milestone:** Seamless integration of all modules into a singular, unified ecosystem.
